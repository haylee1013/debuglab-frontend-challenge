How It Works

A simple workshop rhythm

Students learn best when the session has momentum: demonstrate, make, test, and present.

Phase 01

Brief

Coaches introduce the challenge, safety notes, tools, and success criteria.

Phase 02

Build & Test

Students prototype, run tests, collect feedback, and improve their design.

Phase 03

Showcase

Each student explains what worked, what failed, and what they would improve next.

FAQ

STEM Workshop questions

Are STEM workshops beginner friendly?

Yes. Coaches introduce the tools and guide students through the build step by step.

Will students take something home?

Many sessions include a tangible prototype or digital project. The exact output depends on the selected workshop theme.

Can groups book a custom workshop?

Yes. Schools and parent groups can contact Debug Lab to discuss format, age range, and workshop goals.
