FAQ

Software Development questions

Does my child need coding experience?

No. Students can begin with fundamentals and progress into web, JavaScript, and game projects when they are ready.

Are the classes project-based?

Yes. The track focuses on building visible projects so students can connect concepts to outcomes.

What should students bring?

A laptop is recommended for full coding sessions. The coach can advise based on the selected track.
